## Prompt Rules

- Use original fantasy imagery, not official D&D art direction.
- Preserve character continuity across generated images.
- Preserve important item continuity across generated images.
- Do not include official logos, product names, named settings, or named
  copyrighted characters.
- Prefer clear action and readable staging over abstract atmosphere.
