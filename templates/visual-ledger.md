| --- | --- | --- |
| single_scene | One place, one moment, one main action | Naturalistic tabletop image |
| comic_page | Multiple moments, places, or tactical beats | Clear gutters, ordered panels, no speech bubbles |
| map_or_diagram | Spatial decisions, routes, fog of war | Prioritize legibility over atmosphere |
