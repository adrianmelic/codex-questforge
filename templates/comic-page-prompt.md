# Comic Page Prompt

Use this when one response covers multiple places, times, or tactical beats and
a single physical scene would collapse events incorrectly.

```text
Original 5E-compatible fantasy comic page, unofficial and not using official
D&D settings, logos, product art, or named copyrighted characters.

Title: <scene title>.
Format: <2 | 4 | 6> ordered panels with clear gutters.

Panel order:
1. <first place/time/action>.
2. <second place/time/action>.
3. <optional third place/time/action>.
4. <optional fourth place/time/action>.

Continuity: <visible states from visual-ledger.md, such as bandaged left hand,
current outfit, persistent item scale, NPC markings, room layout>.
Style: <campaign style plus comic page; clear staging; no readable text>.
Avoid: speech bubbles, captions, readable lettering, merged timelines,
teleporting NPCs, official logos, copied product art.
Post-generation review: reject if moments from different panels are merged into
one physical location.
```
