The Lantern Under Blackwater Pier

Rain needles the old harbor stones. Three amber lanterns burn beneath the pier, even though the tide should have drowned them hours ago. The harbor master presses a sealed ferry token into your palm and says, "If the light answers you, do not promise it your name."

Choose an action. In the full plugin, Codex keeps this state in local files, rolls openly, updates your campaign journal, and can generate the next image frame.

Play In Codex

Install the plugin, open a new thread, and talk naturally. Codex acts as DM while scripts keep rules, dice, state, and campaign continuity grounded.

Persistent Campaigns

Questforge writes game state, session logs, checkpoints, player journals, and DM-only adventure spines so a story can continue after a long pause.

Visual Table Feel

Static scene images can appear in chat for mobile play, while local galleries and 360 viewers give desktop sessions a stronger sense of place.
